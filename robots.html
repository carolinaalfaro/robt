Robot Events

Throughout the semester in the computer science pathway field, students spend a period of time working on robots. In pairs, the computer scientists worked with the GoPiGos robots and learned to understand Python language in order to complete a variety of challenges. Some of the projects presented to them involve competing in a sumo battle, obstacle course, dance battle, and several others. Each competition was unique, creative, uniformed, and entertaining. When writing the robot code, the students gain knowledge of different attributes, in where made informational posters for a school event.
